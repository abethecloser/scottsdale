# Editing Your Website

Welcome! You can update your own website whenever you like, no coding needed.
Everything happens on one simple screen.

## Logging in

Go to your editing screen: **https://<your-studio>.sanity.studio**
(we'll send you the exact link). Log in with the email we invited.

You'll see a menu on the left with things you can edit:

- **Site Settings** — your phone, email, hours, the big headline photo, and the
  three stat numbers.
- **Programs** — your services (each has its own page).
- **Blog Posts** — your articles.
- **Coaches / Team** — the people on your team.
- **Testimonials** — client quotes.
- **Case Studies** — result stories.
- **Gallery Photos** — the "inside the room" photos.

## Making a change

1. Click an item on the left (for example **Site Settings** to change your phone
   number, or **Blog Posts** to write an article).
2. Edit the boxes like a form. To add a photo, click the image box and drag one
   in from your computer.
3. Click **Publish** (bottom right).
4. Your live website updates by itself in about **1–2 minutes**. Refresh your
   site to see it.

## Writing a blog post

1. Click **Blog Posts** → **Create new**.
2. Fill in the title, a short summary (excerpt), a cover photo, and your article
   in the big text box.
3. Pick an **Author** (one of your coaches) and a publish date.
4. Set **Status** to **Published** when you're ready (leave it on **Draft** to
   keep working without it showing on the site).
5. Click **Publish**.

## Tips

- **Draft vs Published:** a post only appears on your website when its Status is
  **Published**. Drafts are private to you.
- **Order:** the "Order" number decides what shows first (lower = first).
- **Photos:** always add the "alt text" (a short description of the photo) — it
  helps Google and visually-impaired visitors.
- Nothing you do here can break the design or layout — you're only changing
  words and pictures. Edit freely.

Questions? Just reach out and we'll help.
